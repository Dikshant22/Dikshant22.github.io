Professional Disinfection & Cleaning Service

We provide hospital-grade professional disinfection service for offices, residential areas, shops, childcare centres etc. Our treatment is proven and disinfects against 99.99% of harmful viruses, bacterial, germs and any common virus. The solution is child-safe, non-toxic, non-allergic and poses no risk to humans or the environment.

Studies have shown that bacteria can stay on the surface of any object and still be active for up to 24 hours. Our sanitization treatment can protect any surface it is sprayed or applied on and reaches even hard-to-reach areas. Our disinfectant has chemical particles that forms a protective layer over surfaces that will kill any virus that comes into contact with it. Moreover, our disinfectant has been put through rigorous lab testing and has even been approved.

Our cold fogging machine uses large volumes of air at low pressures to transform liquid into droplets to be dispersed. This ultra-low volume (ULV) fogging generate a fog/mist formed of droplets between 5 to 50 microns (μm) in diameter. Research has shown that droplets of this size are ideal to tackle pathogens. Our antimicrobial mist treatment is colourless, works like magic and even helps to eliminate undesired odour. This mist can disinfect the microorganisms such as bacteria, germs, viruses in the air.

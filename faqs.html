Do I need to provide the cleaning products?

No. We supply all the necessary cleaning products and equipment. We use products that are environmentally sound and certified that out performs most common cleaning chemical products.

Do I have to be home during the clean?

No. If you are not going to be home during the house clean, you can contact your cleaner and arrange access to your home. We highly recommend you meet with your cleaner prior to the service to ensure they understand your needs and requirements.

Do you work on weekends?

At the moment, we provide our services Monday-Saturday.

Can i change my cleaner if I am not satisfied?

Of course. Your satisfaction is important to us and if any part of our service did not meet your expectations please contact us. A replacement will be arranged courteously.

Can you give me an estimate over the phone?

To guarantee an accurate estimate, an in-person inspection is necessary.

What services are suitable for me?

From regular weekly, fortnightly, or monthly services to a one-off for special occasions, we have a range of flexible services available to suit your needs. Whatever your requirements, we can tailor-make a service to fit your busy schedule, budget and needs.

Will I have the same cleaner?

Yes. If you book a regular cleaning service, you will be assigned the same team/cleaner for the duration of your service. If your cleaner is absent, we’ll do our best to find the most suitable replacement. All our cleaners are provided training to ensure that the quality of our service is always consistent.

How do I pay?

Please pay your cleaner on the day by either cash or bank transfer after completion of the service.

All cleaners that are working with BlingHome are covered by Public Liability Insurance. This means that you are protected from injury and damage.
